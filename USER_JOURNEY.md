# Umran | عمران – UI/UX Flow & User Journey

## screens
1. **Landing / Auth**: Minimal onboarding focused on impact.
2. **Main Hub**: 
   - Tab 1: Map (Visual overview).
   - Tab 2: Feed/List (Recent reports).
   - Tab 3: Campaigns (Action center).
3. **Report Form**: 3-step wizard (Capture -> Detail -> Submit).
4. **Issue Detail**: Timeline of execution, before/after gallery.

## User Scenarios
### Scenario A: The Active Citizen
- *Context*: Ahmed finds a broken pipe.
- *Action*: Opens Umran offline. Records a voice note in Sudanese Arabic. Takes a photo.
- *Result*: App saves locally. Syncs when Ahmed reaches home WiFi.

### Scenario B: The Campaign Leader
- *Context*: Sara sees 10 reports for a dirty park.
- *Action*: Starts a "Clean-up Friday" campaign linked to the issue.
- *Result*: Neighborhood members get notified, join the event, and post photos of the clean park.
