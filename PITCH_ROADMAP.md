# Umran | عمران – Pitch & Roadmap

## Pitch Summary
"Umran is the operating layer for participatory infrastructure. We turn fragmented community observations into data-driven municipal decisions, enabling low-cost, high-impact maintenance in regions with traditionally weak data infrastructure."

## Roadmap
- **Q2 2026**: MVP Launch in Khartoum & Omdurman (Pilot).
- **Q3 2026**: SMS-to-Issue integration for non-smartphone users.
- **Q4 2026**: Predictive analytics (using historical report clusters to predict pipe bursts).
- **Q1 2027**: Private sector API for CSR integration.
