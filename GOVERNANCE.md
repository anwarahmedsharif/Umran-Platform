# Umran | عمران – Governance & Data Protection

## 1. Privacy by Design
- **Anonymity**: User UIDs are salt-hashed for public views.
- **Minimization**: Only geolocation and issue metadata are required.
- **Collection Control**: Users can request deletion of their reports and media.

## 2. Ethical AI Usage
- **Gemini Role**: Assistance in translating dialect to standardized data.
- **Human in the Loop**: Final priority decisions are flagged for review by municipal admins, not automated without oversight.

## 3. Compliance Mindset
- **GDPR Alignment**: Data residency (Cloud Run region), encryption at rest (Firebase), and explicit consent during onboarding.
- **Audit Logs**: Every status change on an issue is immutably logged to prevent corruption or bribery at the execution level.
